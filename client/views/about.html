ABOUT HAIR SMOOTHIE BAR

Hair Smoothie Bar began because of a need, a need to help Sheri Johnson treat her daughter's scalp issues. Soon after, her clients began to ask what products she was using at home and wanted her to produce them for her. Sheri believes that your hair should eat a healthy diet just like your body needs. Her vision is to open Hair Smoothie Bars in every city so that everyone can experience benefits of a healthy hair diet. All of the hair smoothies begin with a creamy base, teas, and agave nectar or honey. The produce changes between smoothies depending on your hair needs. All hair smoothies are made-to-order from raw ingredients. The only preservative is your refrigerator!
